## Daily Standup Format (5 min each day)

**Monday (Kickoff):**
- Sprint goal review
- Blockers from last sprint?
- Today's priorities

**Tue-Thu (Regular):**
- What shipped yesterday
- What shipping today
- Blockers (if any)

**Friday (Wrap-up):**
- What shipped this sprint?
- Data Analyst presents weekly metrics snapshot
- Blockers for next sprint
- Wins to celebrate
